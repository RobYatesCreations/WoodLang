üåç Unit Handling

WoodLang recognizes common imperial and metric units and converts all internal measurements automatically. You can:

  • Set a default unit system: units imperial or units metric
  • Override units inline (e.g. 400mm or 24")
  • Export cut lists in either format with conversion handled automatically
